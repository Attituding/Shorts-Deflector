Shorts Deflector
New Pages
Redirect when you click on hyperlinks, reload the page, or open a Shorts video in a new tab or window. You can still use the Shorts item on the YouTube navigation menu normally.
Direct Actions
This option enables redirecting for left click/direct actions, such as clicking on a video with the Shorts icon or the Shorts item on the YouTube navigation menu.
