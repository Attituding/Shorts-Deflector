What is Shorts Deflector? Shorts Deflector is a Chromium and Firefox browser extension that redirects YouTube Shorts to the normal desktop interface.
Why does this extension/add-on exist? YouTube has tried to make Shorts relevant on the desktop too. In these attempts, the mobile-optimized format can become very annoying. For instance, clicking on a Short from a channel's video's page brings up the stupid interface. Another example is that anyone linking you a Short (such as from Discord) will also bring up the Shorts interface, even on a desktop. With Shorts Deflector, you don't have to use the stupid Shorts interface, and get to view them as a normal YouTube video.
How do I use this extension? For Chromium based browsers, such as Chrome, Edge, Opera, Vivaldi, Brave, and more, click here and click add. For Firefox, click here and click Add to Firefox. To configure or manually redirect, start by clicking the Shorts Deflector icon on the extensions hot bar (usually in the top right).
